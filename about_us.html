About Us

ABOUT GOGI

THE STORY

Gogi Asian Restaurant, nestled in the vibrant Maadi area, has quickly become a beloved culinary destination since its establishment three years ago. The restaurant was founded by a group of passionate food enthusiasts who shared a vision of bringing authentic Asian flavours to the local community.

ORIGINS

The name "Gogi" is derived from the Korean word for "meat," reflecting one of the core elements of their menu. The founders were inspired by their travels across Asia, where they experienced a rich tapestry of culinary traditions. They wanted to create a space where people could enjoy dishes from various Asian cuisines, including Korean, Japanese, and Chinese.

The Journey

From the beginning, Gogi aimed to provide not just food, but an experience. The founders sourced high-quality ingredients and partnered with skilled chefs who had expertise in different Asian culinary styles. The restaurant's interior was designed to evoke a warm and inviting atmosphere, combining modern aesthetics with traditional Asian elements.

ABOUT US

Gogi Restaurant is an Asian Fusion restaurant, establishment located at the heart of Maadi, Cairo, Egypt. It offers a variety of traditional Korean dishes with a focus on healthy, flavorful menu options. Korean cuisine is known for its use of fresh vegetables, lean meats, and complex, aromatic seasonings, making it an appealing choice for health-conscious consumers.
